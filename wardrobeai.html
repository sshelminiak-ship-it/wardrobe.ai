How WardrobeAI works

Three effortless steps to a week of beautiful outfits, curated just for you.

01

Upload your clothes

Snap photos of your tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories. The more you upload, the richer the combinations.

02

Set your style

Choose your vibe — casual, business, streetwear, boho — and your current season to get context-perfect outfits.

03

Get your week planned

Our AI vision model analyzes your actual items and creates 7 complete daily looks, complete with stylist tips.

04

Mix & remix

Not feeling Monday? Regenerate with one click. Every plan is unique and always based on what you actually own.

Our story

Built by people who hate
morning wardrobe panic

WardrobeAI started with a simple frustration: standing in front of a full wardrobe and feeling like you have nothing to wear. We built the AI stylist we always wished existed — and made it completely free for everyone.

Our mission

Fashion should be joyful,
not stressful.

We believe that getting dressed should be one of the best parts of your day — a creative act that builds confidence and expresses who you are. The problem isn't your wardrobe; it's having a thoughtful eye to see the possibilities within it. That's exactly what WardrobeAI provides.

By combining the latest AI vision technology with genuine fashion intelligence, we help you rediscover and reimagine the clothes you already own — reducing waste, saving money, and making every morning a little more delightful.
